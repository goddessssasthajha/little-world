18 October 2025, 9:42 a.m.
I got queued with someone named Max Verstappen.
Little did I know that person would end up having such a huge impact on my life.
To be honest, I wasn't really living much of a life back then. But somehow, she made me happier and gave me direction. Not because of something extraordinary she did for me, but simply because she existed.
At first, she was just another person.
Then she became someone I enjoyed talking to.
Then she became someone I started waiting for.
And somehow, without realizing it, she became someone I cared about far more than I ever planned to.
It wasn't one big moment.
It was hundreds of little things.
The way she talks.
The way she laughs.
The random things she gets excited about.
And her voice... genuinely unfair. Some people hear music in songs; I hear it when she talks.
Those little things slowly became my favorite things.
I won't say she comes to my mind every minute.
She came once, and never really left.
She's so much like me in some ways. She isolates herself when she's feeling low, just like I do. The difference is that even when I don't feel like talking to anyone, she's the one person I'd still want to talk to.
And by the way, our Spotify Blend being 99% is honestly disappointing. It should've been 100.
Sometimes I feel like she's too precious to fit inside something as small as a photograph or a screen. I want the whole world to be filled with reminders of her.
This keyboard.
This laptop.
This pillow.
Everywhere I look, somehow there's a thought of her.
If she knew how many times I put aside my own feelings just to understand her perspective, maybe she'd finally realize how lovable she really is.

And I already know what she's probably thinking while reading this:
"I don't deserve this."
ispe toh mai yahi kahunga
"Tumhe toh iss duniya ki saari khushi ka haq hai.
Ek baar haq jata ke toh dekho."
Because if anyone deserves EVERYTHINGGG, it's you.
I want to love you exactly as you are, not as some perfect version of you.
I won't let you go throgh that fear, if you want to run away from everyone i'll run away with you, if you want to live in hiding forever i'll hide with you forever.
And if my arms can't reach the hurt you hide, then I hope my prayers can hold you from the other side.
Lastly, I didn't make this expecting anything from you.
I made it so whenever you feel low and isolate yourself, i hope you will come to this and it may help you to feel a little good.
I hope one day when you feel like running away, you run towards me.
Heal.
Move forward.
Just don't stay trapped in the same chapter forever, because the world deserves to see the astha I see.
thank you.
